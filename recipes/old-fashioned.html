Old Fashioned

The perfect drink after a long, hard day. Reflect on your choices in life while feeling the gentle sting of your whiskey smoothed ever so reassuringly with the flavors of orange and cherry.

Ingredients

  • 2 sugar cubes
  • 4 splashes of orange bitters
  • 4 ounces of bourbon (Buffalo Trace preferred)
  • 2-4 Luxardo cherries
  • 2 Orange peels
  • 2 giant ice cubes (or 6 smaller cubes)

Steps

  1. Add the sugar cubes, orange bitters, and a dash of water to the bottom of a shaker and muddle.
  2. Add the bourbon and a handful of ice.
  3. Shake for approximately 30 seconds.
  4. Add one giant ice cube to each glass (or 3 smaller cubes if giant ice isn't available).
  5. Strain and pour shaker contents over ice, dividing between both glasses.
  6. Squeeze orange peel to release its juices, and coat the rims of each glass with the orange peel juices.
  7. Add orange peel and 1-2 Luxardo cherries to each glass, and enjoy!
