Whiskey Cocoa

It's cold out. Life is hard. You have control over one of these two things. Make life a littler easier on a cold day with whiskey cocoa.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Fireball Whiskey (or another cinnamon whiskey of your choosing)
  • 1 packet of hot cocoa mix (Abuelita preferred)
  • 1 dash orange bitters (optional)
  • Whipped cream and sprinkles for your topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Add boiled water to your hot cocoa mixture, leaving room for your whiskey. Stir.
  2. Add your whiskey and your dash of bitters (if using) Stir again.
  3. Add whipped cream and sprinkles. Enjoy!
