Blueberry Margarita

Try this delicious and refreshing blueberry margarita. It'll make your heart sing on a hot, summer day. The sweetness of the blueberry, together with the tang of the tajin, makes this a sensational treat.

Ingredients

  • Handful of blueberries (either fresh or frozen. If frozen, throw in the microwave for about 20 seconds.
  • Basil or mint sprigs (optional)
  • 1 ounce fresh-squeezed lime juice
  • 2 ounces cointreau (or triple sec)
  • 2.5-3 ounces of tequila (Espolon preferred)
  • Squirt of agave syrup or simple syrup
  • .5 teaspoon tajin
  • .5 teaspoon sugar/sprinkles

Steps

  1. Chill two magratia glasses, then coat the rims with lime juice.
  2. sprinkle the tajin and sugar on a plate, and mix together.
  3. Place each glass upside-down in the sugar and tajin mixture and fully coat the rims.
  4. Place the blueberries, squirt of agave syrup, and basil (if using) in the bottom of a shaker. Muddle until the blueberries have released their juices.
  5. Add the lime juice, cointreau, tequila, and ice.
  6. Shake for about 30 seconds.
  7. Add 3-4 ice cubes to your margarita glasses. Strain and pour your margarita mixture over the ice, and enjoy!
